Ants inspired AI

Javascript School Project

Creation of an AI based on ants' collective intelligence. The main goal is for every ant to find their way back home. They decide where to go based on the amount and type of pheromones located on each neighboring hexagon. Digital pheromones are secreted by each ants. The intensity and type of the pheromones are represented by the fading dots on the map.
